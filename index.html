Our Mission

Where science meets service

The Neuroscience Education and Research Academy (NERA) is a charitable organization created to address the needs of our community. Our aim is to support neurosurgery and neurology research projects and the neurosurgery fellowship training program.

Our research collaboration initiative provides opportunities for students from high school to undergraduate and graduate levels. This platform also allows our community to be involved in research trials. Our community can support our effort through financial donations.

We also provide educational resources — including original surgical procedure videos — produced locally for pure educational purposes, benefiting nursing teams, medical professionals, patients, and the public.

Fellowship Program

Train at the frontier of neurosurgery

General Neurosurgery & Spinal Surgery

We offer fellowship training in general neurosurgery and spinal surgery in Windsor, Ontario. This fellowship provides an excellent opportunity to enhance clinical experience across all aspects of neurosurgery — including complex spine surgery, minimally invasive techniques, neuro-trauma, neuro-oncology, and peripheral nervous system pathologies. Affiliated with Schulich School of Medicine at Western University.

Apply for Fellowship →

What Fellows Experience

Complex spine surgery · Minimally invasive techniques · Neuro-trauma management · Neuro-oncology · Peripheral nervous system pathologies · Surgical education from pre-med through residency
